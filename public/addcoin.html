Add a New Cryptocurrency

Listing a new cryptocurrency on Novixx Exchange incurs a one-time fee of $250 to cover administrative and technical costs.

It will also require a liquidity pool of at least $50 in both the new coin and DGB to ensure smooth trading operations.

Free Listings for Good Causes

We are committed to supporting creative and/or useful projects. If your cryptocurrency project is aimed at benefiting the community or has a charitable aspect, we may consider waiving the listing fee.

Please reach out to us on Discord to discuss your project and explore the possibility of a free listing.
